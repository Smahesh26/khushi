Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, privacy, and support options.

Is my call really confidential?

+

Yes, absolutely. All calls to our helpline are completely confidential. We do not record your phone number, collect personal information, or share your conversation with anyone. Our counselors are trained to provide judgment-free support in a safe, private environment.

What information can you provide?

+

We provide accurate, medically-reviewed information about reproductive healthcare options, your legal rights, and available support services. Our trained counselors can help you understand your choices, connect you with resources, and offer emotional support without judgment.

Is this service available 24/7?

+

Yes, our helpline is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. You can call anytime you need support or information. Our online chat and text services may have specific hours, which will be displayed when you access those options.

Do I need to provide my real name?

+

No, you don't need to provide your real name or any identifying information. You can remain completely anonymous when contacting us. Our goal is to provide support in whatever way makes you feel most comfortable and safe.

What languages do you support?

+

We offer support in multiple languages including English, Hindi, and other regional languages. When you call or chat with us, you can request assistance in your preferred language, and we'll connect you with an appropriate counselor.
